Humidity Sensing Low Cost Passive Chipless RFID Tag

Group design project. The goal was to design a radio-frequency identification device that is easy to manufacture (low cost), with no internal power source (passive), that does not require a microchip (chipless), and that can detect changes in humidity.

The first picture shows our experimental results for different levels of humidity. Our design process is illustrated by the second picture. The third picture is a high level block diagram of the system.
